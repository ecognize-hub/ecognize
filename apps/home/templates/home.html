Ecognize

Ecognize is the first social network and collaboration platform for everybody working in or volunteering to help the planet: No matter if NGOs, law enforcement, academic researchers, intergovernmental organizations, or volunteers.

For volunteers

  • Report: Notify authorities and NGOs about environmental crimes and issues.
  • Connect: Contact other volunteers and organizations in your area. Find friends and use the open forums for collaboration.
  • Act: Find and organize events near you: cleanups, meetups, demonstrations, and many more.

For professionals and researchers

  • Cross-organization collaboration & communication: Private and shared forums where different organizations can meet and exchange information. Both in the same sphere (e.g. all NGOs in one country) and of different types (e.g., NGOs and law enforcement).
  • Volunteer outreach: Engage volunteers and recruit them for events or help.
  • Reporting & monitoring: Receive reports and intelligence from volunteers and local sources.
